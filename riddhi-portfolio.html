Meet Riddhi! 👋

Hi! I'm Riddhi Mukesh Jain, a 5th Standard student at VPNCPS School in Nanded City, Pune. I love learning new things, and this summer I discovered something incredible — Artificial Intelligence!

Through the amazing Knowledge Duckling AI Summer Course, I spent 15 wonderful days learning how to use AI tools to help me study, create art, write stories, compose music, and build my very own website!

My teacher, Tejashri ma'am, taught me that AI is not just for adults — it's for curious kids like me too! Now I can use AI to do homework faster, create beautiful posters, and even write songs! 🎵
